ABOUT US

ABOUT US

EduFort Technologies is a cutting-edge security and attendance monitoring provider, specializing in school safety. With advanced technologies like facial recognition, biometrics, UHF gates, and RFID, we ensure real-time SMS alerts to parents on students' entry, exit, and absenteeism. Our mission is to create a secure, transparent, and connected educational ecosystem.

VISION
